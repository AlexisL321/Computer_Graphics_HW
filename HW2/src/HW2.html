How to play with it:

  1. first set the parameters.
  2. then hit the "Start" button.
  3. then hit "Reset" button to reset the whole thing.
This is a thrower that allows you to change the angle the force applied to throw the object (i.e. the initial speed of the object once thrown). The yellow dot at the end of the canvas is your target. You can see the trajectory once hit "start". Can you find the exact speed and angle to hit the target?
